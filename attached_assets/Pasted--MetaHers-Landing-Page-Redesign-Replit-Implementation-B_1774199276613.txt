# MetaHers Landing Page Redesign — Replit Implementation Brief

---

## OVERVIEW

Redesign the MetaHers Mind Spa landing page to focus on **AI business coaching for women entrepreneurs**. The current page tries to be too many things at once (wellness spa, Web3 education, luxury boat experiences, vision boards, NFTs, digital art) and confuses visitors. The new page has **one clear message**: MetaHers helps women entrepreneurs use AI to grow their businesses, save time, and increase revenue.

**Key strategic shift:**
- OLD positioning: "A Private Sanctuary — Mind Spa for extraordinary women to master AI & Web3"
- NEW positioning: "Use AI to grow your business. Not your workload."

---

## BRAND IDENTITY

**Brand name:** MetaHers
**Tagline:** Use AI to grow your business. Not your workload.
**Target audience:** Women entrepreneurs, freelancers, and small business owners (ages 28–55) who know AI matters but feel overwhelmed by technical resources
**Tone of voice:** Confident, warm, direct. Premium but approachable. Never fluffy or overly spiritual. Think high-end business coaching, not wellness retreat.
**Founder:** Nadia — hospitality executive turned tech entrepreneur and AI coach. Based in Newport Beach, CA. Has coached 300+ women.

## DESIGN SPECIFICATIONS

### Spacing
- Section padding: 7rem top/bottom on desktop, 5rem on mobile
- Container max-width: 1200px with 2rem horizontal padding
- Card border-radius: 20px (large cards), 14px (small floating cards), 100px (pills/buttons)

### Buttons
- Primary: ink background, cream text, pill shape, 1rem 2rem padding
- Secondary: transparent with 1.5px ink border, pill shape
- Hover: translateY(-2px) + subtle shadow
- All buttons use DM Sans, 0.9rem, font-weight 600

### Cards
- Background: white or cream
- Shadow: subtle — `0 1px 3px rgba(0,0,0,0.04), 0 20px 60px rgba(0,0,0,0.06)`
- Hover: lift up 4px + rose-tinted border

### Animations
- Page load: Hero content fades up with staggered delays (0.1s increments)
- Scroll: Feature cards, testimonials, and pricing cards fade up when they enter viewport (IntersectionObserver, threshold 0.15)
- Floating cards: Gentle vertical oscillation (translateY, 6s cycle)
- Nav: Smooth background transition on scroll

### Responsive breakpoints
- Desktop: >1024px — full grid layouts
- Tablet: 768–1024px — two-column grids, single-column hero
- Mobile: <768px — single-column everything, hamburger nav, stacked buttons

## IMPLEMENTATION NOTES

- All anchor links should smooth-scroll to their targets
- The nav should use a scroll listener to toggle a `.scrolled` class at 50px
- Use IntersectionObserver for scroll-triggered fade-in animations on cards
- The hero floating card animation uses CSS `@keyframes float` with translateY
- The pulsing dot in the hero badge uses `@keyframes pulse` on opacity
- All interactive elements need hover states as described
- The page should be fully responsive at 768px and 1024px breakpoints
- No external images are required — all visuals are CSS-based (gradients, avatars with initials, decorative elements)
- When Nadia's photo is available, replace the gradient placeholder in the founder section

## SUMMARY

The redesigned MetaHers landing page has one goal: **convert visitors into free users (Vision Board) and free users into paying members ($49/mo) or private coaching clients ($2,500).**

The page tells a clear story:
1. Here's what we do (Hero)
2. Here's proof it works (Social proof + Testimonials)
3. Here's the problem you have (Problem section)
4. Here's what you get (Features)
5. Here's what it costs (Pricing)
6. Here's who built it (Founder)
7. Start free right now (CTA)

Every section drives toward one of two actions: **Start Free** or **Join Now**.
