## 📚 Lesson 1: AI Photography Mastery

### Create Stunning Brand Photos Without a Camera

**Duration:** 45 minutes

#### What You’ll Learn:

- The AI tools that create the most realistic photos
- How to write prompts that get exactly what you want
- Creating consistent brand photography
- Professional editing tricks using AI

#### Tools You’ll Use:

1. **Midjourney** - For ultra-realistic brand photography
1. **DALL-E 3** (via ChatGPT Plus) - For quick, high-quality images
1. **Leonardo.AI** - For product photos and marketing images
1. **Magnific AI** - For upscaling and enhancement

#### Step-by-Step Tutorial:

**Step 1: Understanding AI Image Prompts**

The secret to great AI photos is in the prompt. Here’s the formula:

```
[Subject] + [Style] + [Lighting] + [Composition] + [Quality Tags]
```

**Example Prompts:**

*For Personal Branding Photos:*

```
Professional Black woman entrepreneur, 30s, sitting at modern desk with laptop, 
natural window lighting, soft focus background, warm tones, confident smile, 
business casual attire, shot on Canon 5D, shallow depth of field, 
professional photography, high resolution, 8k
```

*For Product Photos:*

```
Handmade ceramic mug on white marble countertop, steam rising from coffee, 
morning natural light from window, minimalist kitchen background, 
lifestyle photography, soft shadows, warm color palette, 
commercial photography, 8k, ultra detailed
```

*For Social Media Content:*

```
Flat lay of workspace, laptop, coffee, notebook, flowers in vase, 
overhead shot, bright natural lighting, pink and gold color scheme, 
Instagram aesthetic, professional product photography, clean composition
```

**Step 2: Hands-On Practice**

**Your Assignment:** Create 3 Brand Photos

1. A professional headshot/portrait
1. A workspace/lifestyle shot
1. A product or service visualization

**Tips for Success:**

- Start with simple prompts, then add details
- Save prompts that work well for you
- Create variations by changing one element at a time
- Use the same style keywords for brand consistency

**Step 3: Advanced Techniques**

**Creating Consistency:**

- Use the same reference style in your prompts
- Keep lighting descriptions consistent
- Use “seed” numbers (in Midjourney) to maintain similar results
- Create a prompt template for your brand

**Making It Look Real:**

- Add imperfections: “slight blur,” “natural skin texture”
- Specify camera and lens: “shot on Canon 5D with 50mm lens”
- Include environmental details: “soft window lighting,” “shallow depth of field”
- Avoid AI tells: Skip overly perfect symmetry

- Image 2: Your product/service in action
- Image 3: The transformation/result

## 📚 Lesson 2: AI Video Generation

### Create Scroll-Stopping Videos in Minutes

**Duration:** 60 minutes

#### What You’ll Learn:

- AI video tools that actually work
- Turning images into dynamic videos
- Creating talking head videos without filming
- Adding professional effects and transitions

#### Tools You’ll Use:

1. **Runway ML** - For image-to-video and video editing
1. **HeyGen** - For AI avatar/talking head videos
1. **Pika Labs** - For creative video effects
1. **CapCut** - For AI-powered editing and captions

#### Step-by-Step Tutorial:

**Part 1: Image-to-Video Magic**

**Using Runway ML:**

1. **Upload Your AI Image** (from Lesson 1)
1. **Add Motion Prompts:**
- “Camera slowly zooms in on face”
- “Gentle hair movement from breeze”
- “Steam rising from coffee cup”
- “Subtle head turn and smile”
1. **Adjust Settings:**
- Duration: 4-5 seconds
- Motion strength: Medium (3-5)
- Camera movement: Subtle

**Part 2: AI Talking Head Videos (HeyGen)**

Perfect for:

- Course content
- Product explanations
- Social media tips
- Brand introductions

**How to Create:**

1. **Choose Your Avatar** (or create custom from your photo)
1. **Write Your Script** (30-60 seconds max for social)
1. **Select Voice** (natural, conversational tone)
1. **Add Background** (your brand colors or imagery)
1. **Generate & Download**

**Script Formula for Social Media:**

```
Hook (first 3 seconds): "Want to know the secret to [desired outcome]?"
Value (middle 20 seconds): "Here's exactly what I do..."
CTA (last 5 seconds): "Save this and try it yourself!"
```

**Part 3: Video Editing with AI (CapCut)**

**Auto-Captions:**

- Upload video
- Click “Auto Captions”
- Customize style (use bold, dynamic fonts)
- Match your brand colors

**AI Effects:**

- Auto b-roll insertion
- Transitions based on content
- Background removal
- Speed adjustments for emphasis

## 📚 Lesson 3: AI Copywriting That Converts

### Write Like a Pro Without Staring at a Blank Page

**Duration:** 45 minutes

#### What You’ll Learn:

- ChatGPT prompts for different types of copy
- Creating your brand voice with AI
- Email sequences that sell
- Social media captions that engage

#### Tools You’ll Use:

1. **ChatGPT** - Your AI copywriting assistant
1. **Claude** (that’s me! 😊) - For longer-form content
1. **Copy.ai** - For quick marketing copy variations
1. **Jasper** - For SEO-optimized content (optional)

#### The Ultimate ChatGPT Copywriting Prompts

**For Sales Pages:**

```
You are an expert copywriter specializing in [your industry]. 
Write compelling sales copy for [product/service] that:

Target Audience: [describe your ideal client]
Pain Points: [what problems do they have]
Transformation: [what results do they want]
Unique Angle: [what makes you different]

Use the PAS framework (Problem-Agitate-Solution).
Write in a conversational, empowering tone.
Include emotional triggers and specific benefits.
End with a strong call-to-action.
```

**For Email Sequences:**

```
Create a 5-email welcome sequence for new subscribers to [your business].

Email 1: Welcome + set expectations
Email 2: Share your story + build connection
Email 3: Provide massive value (free resource)
Email 4: Social proof + testimonials
Email 5: Soft pitch for [product/service]

Each email should be 150-200 words, conversational tone, 
with clear subject lines and CTAs.
```

**For Social Media Captions:**

```
Write 10 Instagram captions for [your niche] that:
- Start with a scroll-stopping hook
- Provide value or insight
- End with engagement question
- Include relevant hashtags
- Are 100-150 words each
- Match this brand voice: [describe your voice]

Make them authentic, relatable, and actionable.
```

**For Product Descriptions:**

```
Write a compelling product description for [product name].

Features: [list features]
Benefits: [how it helps customer]
Target Customer: [who it's for]

Write in a way that:
- Focuses on transformation, not just features
- Uses sensory language
- Addresses objections
- Creates urgency
- Is 100-150 words
```

#### Creating Your Brand Voice Guide

**Prompt to Define Your Voice:**

```
Help me define my brand voice for [business name] in [industry].

My personality: [adjectives that describe you]
My values: [what you stand for]
My audience: [who you serve]
What I want to be known for: [your expertise]

Create a brand voice guide that includes:
1. Voice attributes (3-5 adjectives)
2. Do's and Don'ts
3. Example phrases I should/shouldn't use
4. Tone variations for different content types
```

#### Your Copywriting Assignment:

Create this content using AI:

1. **One sales page section** (200 words) for your offer
1. **Three social media captions** for this week
1. **One email** to your audience

**Pro Tip:** Always edit AI copy to add your personality, specific examples, and authentic touches.

## 📚 Lesson 4: Social Media Content Strategy

### Never Run Out of Content Ideas Again

**Duration:** 45 minutes

#### What You’ll Learn:

- Creating a 30-day content calendar with AI
- Repurposing one piece of content 10 ways
- Viral content frameworks
- Scheduling and automating posts

#### The AI Content Calendar System

**Step 1: Generate Content Pillars**

**ChatGPT Prompt:**

```
I'm a [your business type] serving [target audience].

Create 5 content pillars for my social media that:
- Showcase my expertise
- Provide value to my audience
- Drive engagement
- Support my business goals
- Are sustainable to create

For each pillar, explain what types of posts fit within it.
```

**Step 2: Create 30-Day Content Calendar**

**ChatGPT Prompt:**

```
Using these content pillars: [list from above]

Create a 30-day Instagram content calendar with:
- Mix of formats (posts, reels, stories, carousels)
- Balanced across all 5 pillars
- Specific post ideas with hooks
- Best posting times for [your timezone]
- Hashtag strategy

Format as a table with: Date | Content Pillar | Format | Topic | Hook | Hashtags
```

**Step 3: The Repurposing Framework**

Take ONE piece of content and turn it into:

1. **Instagram Post** - Key insight with visual
1. **Instagram Reel** - Short video version
1. **Instagram Carousel** - Step-by-step breakdown
1. **Story Series** - Behind-the-scenes
1. **LinkedIn Post** - Professional angle
1. **Twitter Thread** - Bite-sized tips
1. **Pinterest Pin** - Visual infographic
1. **Email Newsletter** - Deep dive
1. **Blog Post** - Long-form
1. **YouTube Short** - Video snippet

**AI Repurposing Prompt:**

```
I have this content: [paste your original content]

Repurpose it into:
- An Instagram carousel (10 slides)
- A 60-second reel script
- A LinkedIn post
- A Twitter thread (5 tweets)
- An email newsletter

Adapt the tone and format for each platform while keeping the core message.
```

#### Viral Content Frameworks

**Framework 1: The Transformation Post**

- Before: Where they are now
- During: The struggle/journey
- After: The result
- How: Your solution

**Framework 2: The Listicle**

- “5 Things I Wish I Knew About [topic]”
- Number-driven with specific tips
- Each point is actionable

**Framework 3: The Story Hook**

- Start with unexpected statement
- Share relatable story
- Reveal the lesson
- Apply to their life

**Framework 4: The Myth-Buster**

- Common belief: “Most people think…”
- The truth: “But here’s what really…”
- The proof: “Here’s why…”
- The action: “So instead, do this…”

1. **Generate a 7-day content calendar**
1. **Create one piece of content** and repurpose it 3 ways
1. **Use AI to design graphics** for your posts

## 💪 Overcoming Week 1 Challenges

**“The AI photos don’t look like me!”**

- Use reference images with detailed descriptions
- Try different tools (each has strengths)
- Focus on brand aesthetic over perfect likeness
- Consider HeyGen for actual video of “you”

**“I’m overwhelmed by all the tools!”**

- Pick ONE tool per category to start
- Master it before moving to others
- You don’t need all of them
- Free versions are fine to start

**“My content isn’t getting engagement!”**

- It takes time - keep going
- Focus on value, not perfection
- Engage with others first
- Use the viral frameworks
- Ask the community for feedback

**“I don’t know what to create!”**

- Use the content calendar prompts
- Look at what’s working in your niche
- Share YOUR unique perspective
- Document your learning journey
