**Duration:** 60 minutes

#### The Best No-Code Website Builders

**1. Wix (Best for Beginners)**

- **Pros:** Drag-and-drop, AI design assistant, beautiful templates
- **Cons:** Less flexibility once published, Wix branding on free plan
- **Best For:** Small businesses, portfolios, simple stores
- **Price:** Free (with ads) or $16-$45/month
- **AI Feature:** Wix ADI creates entire site from your answers

**2. Squarespace (Best for Creatives)**

- **Pros:** Stunning templates, integrated e-commerce, mobile-optimized
- **Cons:** Learning curve, limited customization
- **Best For:** Photographers, designers, coaches, boutiques
- **Price:** $16-$65/month
- **AI Feature:** Design Intelligence suggests layouts

**3. Framer (Best for Modern Design)**

- **Pros:** Cutting-edge designs, animations, fast loading
- **Cons:** Steeper learning curve, newer platform
- **Best For:** Tech startups, agencies, modern brands
- **Price:** Free or $5-$30/month
- **AI Feature:** AI copywriting and layout suggestions

**4. Webflow (Best for Advanced/Custom)**

- **Pros:** Complete design control, powerful CMS, professional
- **Cons:** Biggest learning curve, more complex
- **Best For:** Agencies, developers, complex sites
- **Price:** $14-$39/month
- **AI Feature:** AI-powered responsive design

**5. Carrd (Best for Simple One-Pagers)**

- **Pros:** Super simple, extremely affordable, fast
- **Cons:** Limited to one-page sites on free plan
- **Best For:** Landing pages, personal sites, link-in-bio
- **Price:** Free or $19/year (yes, per YEAR!)
- **AI Feature:** Limited, but templates are optimized

**Nadia’s Recommendations:**

**If you’re building:**

- **Your first website ever:** Wix or Carrd
- **A creative portfolio:** Squarespace
- **A modern tech brand:** Framer
- **Multiple client websites:** Webflow
- **A quick landing page:** Carrd

#### Domain Names & Hosting Basics

**Choosing Your Domain Name:**

**Best Practices:**

- Keep it short (under 15 characters)
- Easy to spell and say
- Matches your brand name
- Use .com if available (or .co, .io for modern tech)
- Avoid numbers and hyphens

**AI Domain Name Generator Prompt:**

```
Suggest 10 domain name options for my business.

Business Name: [your business name]
Industry: [your industry]
Brand Personality: [adjectives]
Target Audience: [who you serve]
Keywords: [relevant keywords]

For each suggestion, provide:
- The domain name
- Why it works
- Availability likelihood
- Alternative extensions (.com, .co, .io)

Prioritize short, memorable, brandable names.
```

**Where to Buy Domains:**

- **Namecheap** - Affordable, clean interface ($10-15/year)
- **Google Domains** - Simple, Google integration ($12/year)
- **GoDaddy** - Popular but upsell-heavy ($12-20/year)
- **Porkbun** - Budget-friendly ($8-12/year)

**Note:** Most website builders include free domain for first year!

## 📚 Lesson 2: Homepage Design That Converts

**Duration:** 90 minutes

#### The Homepage Formula

**Section 1: Hero (Above the Fold)**
What visitors see first - makes or breaks everything!

**Must Include:**

- **Headline:** Clear value proposition (what you do + who you help)
- **Subheadline:** How you help (the transformation)
- **Hero Image/Video:** Professional, on-brand visual
- **Primary CTA:** One clear action (Book Call, Shop Now, Learn More)
- **Trust Indicators:** Awards, “As Seen On,” client logos

**Hero Section Examples:**

*For a Coach:*

```
Headline: "Transform Your Life in 90 Days"
Subheadline: "Join 500+ women who've built successful businesses 
while maintaining work-life balance"
CTA: "Book Your Free Strategy Call"
Visual: Professional photo of you or happy client
```

*For a Product Business:*

```
Headline: "Skincare That Actually Works"
Subheadline: "Clean, science-backed formulas that deliver 
visible results in 30 days or your money back"
CTA: "Shop Bestsellers"
Visual: Product photography with lifestyle context
```

*For a Service Provider:*

```
Headline: "Websites That Turn Visitors Into Customers"
Subheadline: "Custom web design for entrepreneurs who want 
to stand out and scale without the tech overwhelm"
CTA: "View Portfolio"
Visual: Website mockups or dramatic before/after
```

**AI Hero Section Generator:**

```

Business: [your business name]
What I Do: [your service/product]
Who I Help: [target audience]
Main Benefit: [primary transformation/result]
Unique Differentiator: [what makes you different]

Please provide:
1. A compelling headline (under 10 words)
2. A subheadline that expands on the promise
3. Primary CTA button text
4. Secondary CTA option (if needed)
5. Trust elements to include
6. Recommended hero image concept

Make it clear, compelling, and conversion-focused.
```

-----

**Section 2: Problem/Agitation**
Connect with pain points - show you understand them!

**Structure:**

- Identify their struggle
- Agitate the pain (what happens if they don’t solve it)
- Position yourself as the solution

**Example:**

```
"Tired of spending hours creating content that gets zero engagement?

You're posting daily, but crickets. Your competitors seem to have it 
figured out, while you're stuck wondering what you're doing wrong.

The truth? It's not about posting more—it's about posting smarter 
with AI-powered strategies that actually work."
```

-----

**Section 3: Solution/Offer**
Present your solution clearly!

**Include:**

- What you offer
- How it works (3-5 simple steps)
- What makes it different
- The transformation

**Example Format:**

```
How [Your Solution] Works:

Step 1: [Discovery/Assessment]
Brief description of first step

Step 2: [Implementation/Action]
Brief description of second step

Step 3: [Results/Transformation]
Brief description of outcome
```

-----

**Section 4: Social Proof**
Build trust with testimonials and results!

**Types of Proof:**

- **Testimonials:** 3-5 powerful quotes with photos
- **Case Studies:** Before/after results
- **Numbers:** “500+ Clients Served” “4.9★ Rating”
- **Media Mentions:** Logos of where you’ve been featured
- **Certifications:** Credentials and training

**Testimonial Format:**

```
"[Specific result or transformation they experienced]"

[Pain point they had before]

[Solution you provided]

[Current state/success]

- Client Name, Title/Business
  [Photo]
```

-----

**Section 5: Features/Benefits**
Highlight what makes you special!

**Use Icons + Short Copy:**

- ✓ Feature → Benefit
- ✓ Feature → Benefit
- ✓ Feature → Benefit

**Example:**

```
✓ AI-Powered Tools → Create content 10x faster
✓ Live Support → Get answers in under 2 hours
✓ Lifetime Access → Learn at your own pace
```

-----

**Section 6: Final CTA**
Give them one last chance to convert!

**Strong CTA Structure:**

- Restate the main benefit
- Address final objection
- Clear action button
- Risk reversal (guarantee/free trial)

**Example:**

```
Ready to Transform Your Business with AI?

Join 500+ women who've already made the leap.

[Book Your Free Strategy Call]

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee • No Credit Card Required
```

-----

**Section 7: Footer**
Professional closing with navigation!

**Include:**

- Navigation links
- Contact information
- Social media icons
- Legal pages (Privacy, Terms)
- Newsletter signup
- Copyright notice

#### SEO 101: Getting Found on Google

**What is SEO?**
Search Engine Optimization = Making your website show up when people search on Google

**The 3 Pillars of SEO:**

**1. On-Page SEO** (What’s on your website)

- Keywords in right places
- Quality content
- Good user experience

**2. Technical SEO** (How your site is built)

- Fast loading speed
- Mobile-friendly
- Secure (HTTPS)
- Clean structure

**3. Off-Page SEO** (External signals)

- Backlinks from other sites
- Social media presence
- Online mentions

#### Keyword Research for Beginners

**What Are Keywords?**
Words/phrases people type into Google

**Finding Your Keywords:**

**Step 1: Brain Dump**
List everything related to your business:

- Services you offer
- Problems you solve
- Questions clients ask
- Industry terms

**Step 2: Use AI for Keyword Ideas**

```
Generate SEO keyword ideas for my business.

Business: [your business]
Services/Products: [what you offer]
Target Audience: [who you serve]
Location: [if local business]

Please provide:
1. 10 main keywords (high-level, competitive)
2. 20 long-tail keywords (specific, easier to rank for)
3. 10 question-based keywords (what people ask)
4. Search intent for each (informational/commercial/transactional)
5. Which keywords to target on which pages

Focus on keywords a new website can realistically rank for.
```

**Step 3: Check Keywords (Free Tools)**

- **Google Search:** Type keyword, see what autocompletes
- **People Also Ask:** See related questions
- **AnswerThePublic.com:** Visual keyword suggestions
- **Ubersuggest:** Free keyword difficulty checker

**Step 4: Choose Your Target Keywords**

- **Homepage:** 1-2 main keywords
- **Each Service Page:** 1 specific keyword
- **Blog Posts:** 1 long-tail keyword each

#### On-Page SEO Optimization

**Where to Put Your Keywords:**

**1. Page Title (Most Important!)**

- Include main keyword near the beginning
- Keep under 60 characters
- Make it compelling

**Examples:**

- ❌ “Home | Jane’s Coaching”
- ✅ “Life Coach for Women Entrepreneurs | Transform Your Business”

**2. Meta Description**

- 150-160 characters
- Include keyword naturally
- Compelling call-to-action

**Example:**

```
"Transform your business with AI-powered strategies. 
Join 500+ women entrepreneurs who've 10x'd their productivity. 
Book your free strategy call today."
```

**3. Headers (H1, H2, H3)**

- **H1:** One per page, includes main keyword
- **H2:** Section headers, variations of keyword
- **H3:** Subsections, related terms

**4. URL Structure**

- ❌ `yoursite.com/page1234?id=5`
- ✅ `yoursite.com/ai-coaching-for-women`

**5. Image Alt Text**

- Describe image
- Include keyword naturally
- Good for accessibility too!

**Example:**

```
Alt text: "Woman entrepreneur using AI tools on laptop for content creation"
```

**6. Body Content**

- Use keyword naturally 2-3 times
- Use related terms (synonyms)
- Write for humans, not robots!
- Aim for 300+ words per page

#### AI SEO Optimization Prompt

```
Optimize this webpage content for SEO.

Page: [Homepage/About/Services/etc.]
Target Keyword: [your main keyword]
Current Content: [paste your draft content]

Please provide:
1. Optimized page title (under 60 chars)
2. Meta description (150-160 chars)
3. H1 headline
4. 3-5 H2 subheadlines
5. Revised content with keyword naturally integrated
6. 5 related keywords to sprinkle throughout
7. Suggested URL slug

Make sure it reads naturally and is compelling to humans!
```

#### Technical SEO Checklist

**Before Launch, Verify:**

**Speed & Performance:**

- [ ] Images compressed (use TinyPNG.com)
- [ ] Videos embedded (not uploaded directly)
- [ ] Minimal plugins/widgets
- [ ] Test speed with PageSpeed Insights

**Mobile Optimization:**

- [ ] Responsive design (looks good on phones)
- [ ] Text is readable without zooming
- [ ] Buttons are tappable (not too small)
- [ ] Test on actual phone

**Security:**

- [ ] SSL certificate installed (HTTPS)
- [ ] Secure contact forms
- [ ] Privacy policy page
- [ ] Cookie consent (if applicable)

**Structure:**

- [ ] Clear navigation menu
- [ ] Internal links between pages
- [ ] Sitemap submitted to Google
- [ ] Google Analytics installed

**Content:**

- [ ] No broken links
- [ ] All images have alt text
- [ ] Contact info is accurate
- [ ] Copyright year is current

#### Setting Up Google Tools

**Google Search Console (Free)**
Tells you how your site performs in Google

**Setup Steps:**

1. Go to search.google.com/search-console
1. Add your website
1. Verify ownership (follow instructions)
1. Submit your sitemap
1. Monitor performance weekly

**Google Analytics (Free)**
Shows who visits your site

**Setup Steps:**

1. Go to analytics.google.com
1. Create account
1. Add your website
1. Get tracking code
1. Install on your site (your platform has easy integration)
1. Verify it’s working
