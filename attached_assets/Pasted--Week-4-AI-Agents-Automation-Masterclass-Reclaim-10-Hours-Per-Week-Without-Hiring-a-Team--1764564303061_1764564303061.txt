#### What is Workflow Automation?

**The Concept:**
When [Trigger] happens in App A, automatically do [Action] in App B

**Example:**

- When someone fills out your contact form →
  Automatically add them to your email list →
  Send welcome email →
  Create task in your project manager →
  Notify you in Slack

**No coding required!** Visual workflow builders connect your apps.

#### Zapier vs Make (Integromat)

**Zapier (Easier, Recommended for Beginners)**

- **Pros:** 6,000+ app integrations, very user-friendly
- **Cons:** More expensive, limited on free plan
- **Price:** Free (5 Zaps), $19-69+/month
- **Best For:** Simple automations, beginners

**Make (formerly Integromat) (More Powerful)**

- **Pros:** Visual builder, more free operations, cheaper
- **Cons:** Steeper learning curve
- **Price:** Free (1,000 operations), $9-29+/month
- **Best For:** Complex workflows, advanced users

**Start with Zapier**, move to Make when you need more power.

#### Step-by-Step: Creating Your First Zap

**Example: Website Form → Email List + Notification**

**Step 1: Sign Up for Zapier**

- Go to Zapier.com
- Create free account
- Click “Create Zap”

**Step 2: Set Up Trigger**

1. **Choose Trigger App:** (e.g., Google Forms, Typeform, Website Form)
1. **Choose Trigger Event:** “New Form Response”
1. **Connect Your Account:** Login to that app
1. **Test Trigger:** Zapier finds a recent form submission
1. **Review Data:** See all the fields (name, email, etc.)

**Step 3: Add First Action**

1. **Choose Action App:** (e.g., Mailchimp, ConvertKit, etc.)
1. **Choose Action Event:** “Add/Update Subscriber”
1. **Connect Your Account**
1. **Map Fields:**
- Email → Form Email Field
- First Name → Form Name Field
- Tags → “Website Lead”
1. **Test Action:** Zapier creates test subscriber

**Step 4: Add Second Action (Optional)**

1. **Click “+ Add Step”**
1. **Choose App:** “Email by Zapier” (or Gmail)
1. **Choose Event:** “Send Email”
1. **Set Up Email:**
- To: Your email
- Subject: “🎉 New Lead: [Name from form]”
- Body: “New form submission from [name] ([email])”
1. **Test Action**

**Step 5: Name and Turn On**

1. **Name Your Zap:** “Website Form → Email List + Notification”
1. **Click “Publish”**
1. **Test with real form submission**
1. **Verify it worked!**

**You’re now AUTOMATED!** 🎉

#### Advanced Segmentation

**Don’t send the same email to everyone!**

**Segment By:**

**1. Engagement Level**

- **Active:** Opened last 3 emails → Send new content
- **Warming:** Opened 1-2 emails → Re-engagement content
- **Cold:** No opens in 30 days → Win-back campaign or remove

**2. Purchase History**

- **Customers:** Product updates, upsells, VIP content
- **Non-customers:** Nurture, case studies, offers

**3. Interests/Tags**

- Tagged “Interested in [Product A]” → Product A sequence
- Tagged “Beginner” → Beginner-friendly content
- Tagged “Advanced” → Advanced strategies

**4. Behavior-Based**

- Clicked link about [Topic] → More content on that topic
- Abandoned cart → Cart recovery sequence
- Downloaded [Resource] → Related content sequence
