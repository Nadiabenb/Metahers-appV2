You are a senior full‑stack engineer. Extend the existing Replit app “MetaHers Mind Spa” by adding a new module called **AI Glow‑Up Program** — a 14‑day automated experience that teaches women branding, niche, voice, social‑media mastery, and building‑in‑public using AI.

---

## ARCHITECTURE

- Framework: **Next.js 14 (App Router)** + **TypeScript**
- Styling: **Tailwind CSS**
- State: **Zustand**
- Storage: **localStorage** (MVP) or Replit DB fallback
- Folder path: `/app/glow-up`
- Add navigation item: “Glow‑Up Program”

---

## ROUTES

1. `/glow-up`
   - Onboarding form:
     - name  
     - brand type (personal / business)  
     - niche  
     - main platform (IG / TikTok / LinkedIn / X)  
     - goal (rebrand / new)  
   - Save data and display a personalized welcome message.

2. `/glow-up/dashboard`
   - 14‑day unlock sequence.
   - Each day card includes:
     - **Lesson Block:** short concept (branding, niche, voice, social, public building).  
     - **GPT Prompt:** pre‑filled task.  
     - **Share Template:** public‑post caption (copy button).  
     - **Save Response:** store answers to Journal.  
     - **Mark Done:** unlocks next day.
   - Progress bar across top.

3. `/glow-up/journal`
   - Displays saved GPT outputs + public‑post drafts with edit and copy options.

4. `/glow-up/complete`
   - Auto‑generate **Glow‑Up Summary**:
     - brand voice, tone, keywords  
     - color palette (hex)  
     - short bio + offer statement  
     - 3 content pillars  
   - “Download Brand Report” (HTML export).

### Example content (Agent 3 must generate full 14 sets):

**Day 1 – Your Brand Starts With You**
- Lesson: why self‑awareness and values define a brand.  
- GPT Prompt: “Write your founder story in 3 paragraphs showing why you started.”  
- Caption: “Day 1 of my AI Glow‑Up ✨ Here’s why I built this brand and what it means to me…”

**Day 2 – Find Your Niche**
- Lesson: define target audience + specific result.  
- GPT Prompt: “Describe who you help, their pain points, and the result you deliver.”  
- Caption: “I finally claimed my niche 💫 I help [who] achieve [result] through [method].”

**Day 3 – Craft Your Voice**
- Lesson: tone consistency and cross‑platform voice.  
- GPT Prompt: “Generate 3 brand voice styles (bold, soft, luxurious). Pick one that fits me.”  
- Caption: “I’m shaping my brand voice today — because consistency builds trust.”

Continue for Days 4–14 covering:  
4 Visual Identity • 5 Platform Strategy • 6 Bio Glow‑Up • 7 Storytelling • 8 Offer Design • 9 Content Pillars • 10 Social Engine • 11 Build in Public • 12 Audience Nurture • 13 Consistency Audit • 14 Glow‑Up Recap.

Agent 3 must generate educational text (~100 words each), a practical GPT prompt, and a public‑post caption template for every day.
